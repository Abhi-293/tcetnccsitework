Combined Annual Training Camp (CATC 405) – Army Wing, TCET

Camp Overview

Organised by the 3 Maharashtra Battalion at Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET), CATC 405 brought together cadets from multiple colleges for a dynamic and disciplined military training experience. Activities included drill practice, physical fitness, rifle handling, map reading, obstacle training, and fire safety. The camp also featured sessions on disaster management, hygiene, social service, and national integration. Cultural programs and competitions encouraged team spirit, leadership, and personal growth.
