A Grand Celebration of National Pride

TCET NCC Marks the 76th Republic Day with a Grand Parade

The TCET NCC Unit celebrated the 76th Republic Day with a spectacular parade, showcasing the discipline and patriotism of the cadets. The event was graced by the presence of the Principal, Vice Principal, and other dignitaries. The celebration included a flag hoisting ceremony, the national anthem, and a march past by the NCC cadets.
