Lt. Dr. Nivant Kamble

Associate NCC Officer (ANO), Army Wing – TCET

Rank: Lieutenant

Wing: Army Wing (Senior Division & Senior Wing)
Battalion: 3 Maharashtra Battalion, Mumbai Group ‘A’

Profile Overview

Lt. Dr. Nivant Kamble serves as the Associate NCC Officer (ANO) for the Army Wing at Thakur College of Engineering and Technology (TCET). With a strong academic foundation and unwavering dedication to youth development and national service, he plays a pivotal role in mentoring and guiding NCC cadets through every stage of their journey.

He regularly conducts lectures aligned with the B and C Certificate syllabus, while also integrating defence awareness to help cadets grow intellectually and develop a well-rounded perspective. His sessions are aimed at building knowledge, discipline, and leadership—preparing cadets not just for exams, but for real-life challenges and responsibilities.

Leadership & Impact

In addition to his instructional duties, Lt. Dr. Kamble acts as a vital bridge between the TCET NCC Army Wing and the 3 Maharashtra Battalion, Mumbai Group ‘A’, ensuring seamless communication, coordination, and implementation of NCC activities and official directives.

Known for his disciplined approach and values-driven mentorship, he has led cadets through various prestigious camps including CATC 405. His guidance fosters integrity, service, and civic responsibility among cadets, embodying the NCC motto — “Unity and Discipline”.
