Profile Overview

Lt. Dr. Nivant Kambale

Lt. Dr. Nivant Kambale is a highly accomplished academic, holding a PhD in English Literature from Savitribai Phule Pune University and qualifying UGC-NET. Known for his interdisciplinary work, he explores innovation in pedagogy, blending technology, language, and emotional design in education.

With more than a decade of teaching experience at premier institutions including the National Defence Academy, Dr. Kambale brings a unique perspective to communication and literature. He is a certified Soft Skills and IELTS trainer by the British Council and currently holds the prestigious rank of Lieutenant in the Indian Army through the NCC.

His academic pursuits remain focused on enriching English Language and Literature education, while contributing meaningfully to national service through his role as Associate NCC Officer.
